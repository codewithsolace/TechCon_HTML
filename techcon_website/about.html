Our History

Founded in 2010 by a group of Silicon Valley innovators, TechCon began as a small gathering of 200 technology enthusiasts. What started as a modest one-day event has blossomed into the premier multi-day technology conference, attracting over 15,000 attendees annually.

Over the years, TechCon has been hosted in various tech hubs including San Francisco, Austin, and Boston, with our 2024 edition returning to our roots in the Bay Area.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to bridge the gap between emerging technologies and practical implementation. Our three pillars are:

  • Education: Democratizing access to cutting-edge technical knowledge
  • Innovation: Showcasing groundbreaking technologies before they hit mainstream
  • Connection: Creating meaningful networking opportunities across all levels of the tech industry
